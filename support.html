Quick FAQs

  • What is The Culture?

    A collection of bold social games designed to spark real conversation, connection, and chaos. It's part party, part therapy, part thought lab.

  • How do I play?

    Choose a game, gather your crew, and follow the prompts. Some games have timers, some have phases — all are meant to stir something in you.

  • Is this for deep convos or fun vibes?

    Both. The Culture is designed to shift between funny, intense, vulnerable, wild, and philosophical — sometimes in the same round.

  • Can I play alone?

    Some games like Mental Gym are solo-friendly, but the real magic happens with 2+ people. It’s built for shared experiences.

  • Is there a way to suggest prompts or games?

    Yes! We're all about community fuel. Drop your ideas on our feedback page or DM us on socials.

  • What age group is this for?

    Mostly Gen Z & young adults, but anyone down for raw convos and weird laughs will fit right in. Some games may touch on mature themes.

  • Does it work offline?

    Yes! Once the page is loaded, most games can run without an internet connection. Ideal for dorms, road trips, or Wi-Fi dead zones.

  • Is it free?

    Yup — 100% free for now. We just want you to play and connect. In the future, premium features might drop, but the core will always be open.
